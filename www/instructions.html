Instructions

Setting Up

When prompted, allow camera access to your browser. Then follow the instructions to caliberate your cursor with your eye gaze.

How To Play

Move the ball from start to finish without hitting the maze's walls. Control the ball's direction with the movement of your eye as follows:


  • Up: look at the top center of your screen
  • Down: look at the bottom center of your screen
  • Right: look at the rightmost center of your screen
  • Left: look at the leftmost center of you screen

  • *Tip 1: Press the space bar to toggle eyetracking*
  • *Tip 2: If you get stuck, use the arrow keys to control the ball*
